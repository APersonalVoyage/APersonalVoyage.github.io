    About Me

    Hi, I’m Abhishek, a Data Automation Engineer at Legal and General. I enjoy solving problems, automating processes, and exploring new ideas across different fields.

    Here’s a bit about me: I have an MSc in Gravitational Physics from Cardiff University, earned through a merit-based scholarship, and a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication. My academic background helped me develop strong problem-solving skills and a deep curiosity for understanding complex systems.

    I’ve had the chance to work on some fascinating projects. With Dr. Ian Harrison, I explored Gravitational Wave Constraints on Modified Gravity. I also learned about Parameter Estimation of Gravitational-wave Sources with Dr. Vivian Raymond, where we used tools to analyze data and uncover the secrets of the universe.

    Outside of research, I’ve worked as a Teaching Assistant, helping others learn Computational Physics with Python. My time at ISRO gave me hands-on experience with algorithms and real-world projects.

    I’m skilled in Python, SQL, and Linux, and I’ve worked with tools like TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, GWPY, and Astropy. I’ve also participated in hackathons like the INDIA-SINGAPORE Hackathon 2018 and SMART INDIA HACKATHON 2018, where I learned a lot and had fun solving problems.

    I’ve recently updated my blog to share my thoughts on different topics. Feel free to check it out and reach out if you’d like to chat about ideas from any field. Whether it’s about space, technology, or something completely different, I’d love to hear from you!
