    Hey there! I'm Abhishek Karkola, your go-to guy when it comes to all things Gravitational Physics and Astrophysics. Imagine unraveling the mysteries of the cosmos – that's my passion, and I'm thrilled to have you on this cosmic journey with me. Picture this: I proudly hold an MSc degree in Gravitational Physics from Cardiff University, earning a merit that stands as a testament to my dedication. But that's not all – I've got a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication from PES Institute of Technology under my belt, proving that I've got the brains and the grit to navigate this incredible universe of knowledge. I've been down in the trenches, hands-on with research that'll blow your mind. With Dr. Ian Harrison as my guide, I've dived deep into updating Gravitational Wave Constraints on Modified Gravity. And oh boy, the insights I've uncovered are mind-bending. Thanks to Dr. Vivian Raymond, I've mastered the art of Parameter Estimation of Gravitational-wave Sources – I've dabbled with real-world analysis toolkits, unraveling the secrets hidden in the universe's whispers. Hold onto your hats – I've even ventured into the world of GARBAGE VOLUME ESTIMATION USING MULTI-VIEW PHOTOS, because who said astrophysics can't help clean up our planet? Dr. Shikha Tripathi was my guiding star through this eco-conscious journey. Beyond the books and the lab, I've been in the trenches, putting my knowledge into action. As a Teaching Assistant at Cardiff University, I've helped budding physicists wrap their heads around Computational Physics using Python. And guess what? I've apprenticed at none other than the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). Yep, I've put my skills to the test, implementing cutting-edge algorithms and contributing to projects that have real-world impact. Python and SQL? Oh, you bet I've got 'em in my toolkit. I've danced with Linux distributions, tangoed with High Performance Computing (HPC), and mastered the art of crafting Convolutional Neural Network models. Libraries like Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, GWPY, Astropy, and PYCBC? They're my trusted companions on this incredible journey. Buckle up, because my coursework includes everything from General Relativity and Cosmology to Numerical Relativity and Data Analysis. And I'm not just about the academics – I've taken my talents to hackathons, securing victories in prestigious competitions like INDIA-SINGAPORE Hackathon’2018 and SMART INDIA HACKATHON’2018. Hey, don't just take my word for it. You're welcome to reach out to Dr. Ian Harrison, Prof. Paul Roche, and Dr. Richard Lewis, my esteemed mentors, who can vouch for my passion and expertise in this captivating cosmic realm. Let's journey through the stars together – stick around for more mind-bending insights and discoveries that'll keep you at the edge of your seat!
