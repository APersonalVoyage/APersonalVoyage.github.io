    About Me

    I'm a Data Automation Engineer at Legal and General, passionate about problem-solving, automating workflows, and exploring ideas across various domains.

    Academically, I hold an MSc in Gravitational Physics from Cardiff University, awarded through a merit-based scholarship, along with a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication. My studies have honed my analytical skills and deepened my curiosity about complex systems.

    I've worked on intriguing research projects, including investigating Gravitational Wave Constraints on Modified Gravity with Dr. Ian Harrison and learning about Parameter Estimation of Gravitational-wave Sources with Dr. Vivian Raymond. These experiences allowed me to analyze gravitational wave data and uncover insights about the most interesting wave in the universe.

    Beyond research, I've contributed as a Teaching Assistant, guiding students through Computational Physics using Python. My time at ISRO provided valuable hands-on experience in algorithm development and real-world applications.

    I have expertise in Python, SQL, and Linux, and I've worked with tools like TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, GWPY, and Astropy during my degree. Now that I working away from Academia, I have had the opportunity to learn and contribute in Snowflake, AWS's Data Engineering services, DBT tool.

    Additionally, I've participated in hackathons such as the INDIA-SINGAPORE Hackathon 2018 and SMART INDIA HACKATHON 2018, which were incredible learning experiences.
