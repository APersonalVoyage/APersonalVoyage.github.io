    About Me

    Hey, I'm Abhishek Karkola – your space enthusiast diving headfirst into the wild world of Gravitational Physics and Astrophysics. Think of me as your cosmic tour guide, showing you the coolest parts of the universe! So, picture this: I snagged a pretty cool merit-based MSc in Gravitational Physics from Cardiff University, and I'm not just about the fancy degrees. I also rocked a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication. Basically, I'm a space geek with some serious credentials! Now, let's talk research. Dr. Ian Harrison and I teamed up to unravel the secrets behind Gravitational Wave Constraints on Modified Gravity. Trust me, it's like cracking open a treasure chest of cosmic mysteries. And shoutout to Dr. Vivian Raymond – they showed me the ropes of Parameter Estimation of Gravitational-wave Sources, where we decode the universe's whispers using real-world tools. Pretty awesome, right? But hey, I'm not just about lab coats and textbooks. I've been out there, getting my hands dirty. As a Teaching Assistant, I helped fellow space enthusiasts make sense of Computational Physics using Python. And yes, I even hung out with the geniuses at ISRO, where I played around with some seriously cool algorithms and projects that matter. Tech-wise, I'm like a ninja. Python and SQL? You betcha. Linux? Navigating it like a champ. High Performance Computing? Consider it mastered. I've got Convolutional Neural Networks up my sleeve, and I'm besties with tools like Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, GWPY, Astropy, and PYCBC – they're like my cosmic buddies. I've surfed through General Relativity, gotten cozy with Cosmology, and explored more cosmic nooks. But here's the fun part – I'm not just a physics geek. I rocked hackathons like INDIA-SINGAPORE Hackathon’2018 and SMART INDIA HACKATHON’2018, and yeah, I left my mark! Ready to dive into the cosmos with me? Buckle up – we're about to explore stars, galaxies, and maybe even find a portal to another dimension. The universe is our playground, after all!
